Who We Are

About Us

We began as the Laboratory for Cosomological Data Mining and have grown to include more than 20 graduate and undergraduate student researchers from a variety of disciplines including Statistics, Computer Science, Physics, and Astronomy.

UI-Data Science
@UI-DataScience/ds-research-group


Research

Our group explores a diverse range of research topics with a computational flavor. Our current interests include Text Analytics, Cosmological Simulations, Financial Data, and Anomaly Detection.


Teaching

Professor Brunner teaches both Introductory and Advanced data science courses at UIUC through the Informatics department. He also teaches Practical Data Science to professionals at the Research Park.
