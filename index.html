About Us

We began as the Laboratory for Cosomological Data Mining and have grown to include more than 20 graduate and undergraduate student researchers from a variety of disciplines including Statistics, Computer Science, Physics, and Astronomy.

UI-Data Science

Research

Our group explores a diverse range of research topics with a computational flavor. Our current interests include Text Analytics, Cosmological Simulations, Financial Data, and Anomaly Detection.


Teaching

Many courses from departments across campus teach the skills necessary to practice data science. Professor Brunner offers both an Introductory and an Advanced Data Science course through the Informatics Department. He also serves as the Expert in Residence - Data Science at UIUC Research Park where he teaches courses to researchers and professionals who use or want to use the tools of data science in their work.
